Biography

I am pursuing my PhD at the Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). I am working with Prof. Hans van Lint, Prof. Lori Tavasszy and Dr. Maaike Snelder. I am associated with DiTTLab and Freight & Logistics Lab at the TU Delft.

I am interested to improve the reliability of traffic operations between port and hinterland as a part of the ToGRIP project. For these truck dominated motorways, my goal is to design advanced and innovative multi-class traffic management solutions. To achieve this goal, I am delving deeper to understand the behavioral aspects of truck drivers with respect to their route choice and lane changing behavior.
