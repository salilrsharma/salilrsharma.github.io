Biography

I am pursuing my PhD at the Delft University of Technology (TU Delft). I am working with Prof. Hans van Lint, Prof. Lori Tavasszy and Dr. Maaike Snelder. I am associated with DiTTLab and Freight & Logistics Lab at the TU Delft.

I am building data-driven models, advanced control strategies, and next-generation simulation tools to help improve operations and policies of traffic management agencies, trucking companies, the port of Rotterdam, and road users as a part of the ToGRIP project.
