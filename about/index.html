About

Salil Sharma has his Masters degree in Transportation Systems with a specialization in Intelligent Transportation System from the Technical University of Munich, Germany. He obtained his Bachelors degree in Civil Engineering from the Indian Institute of Technology Guwahati (IIT-G) in India. After the bachelor studies, he worked for India's largest exploration and production company, Oil and Natural Gas Corporation Limited where he supervised the construction of a LEED-Platinum rated office complex. For his Masters thesis, he collaborated with GEVAS software GmbH in Munich, Germany on their research project VinstaR. He argued if cost-effective alternatives such as probe vehicle data could replace static detection facilities in online traffic control mechanism. This approach can be useful for small to medium-sized cities which lack detection facilities to monitor current traffic state.
